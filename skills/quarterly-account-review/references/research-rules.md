# QAR Research Rules

## Public Job Requisition Standard

Use only reliable public sources for open software-development related job requisitions. Confirm company identity before including any role. Reliable sources include the company's official careers site, a clearly linked ATS page, or a reputable job listing that identifies the company unambiguously.

## Include

- Software developer, engineer, QA, DevOps, data, AI, automation, platform, mobile, frontend, backend, or similar technical delivery roles.
- Role title and source.
- Only currently open roles when recency can be verified.

## Exclude

- Non-technical roles.
- Ambiguous company matches.
- Expired or stale listings.
- General hiring news without a specific open software-development role.
- Unsupported sales expansion speculation.

## Required Fallback Phrase

If no reliable external software-development job requisition is found, write exactly:
`No external job requistion found`
